UTKESA

University of Toronto Korean Engineering Student Association

Korean Engineering Students' Association (KESA) is a recognized student-run organization at the University of Toronto. Founded in 1981, KESA has dedicated itself in promoting Korean culture, excellence in academics, and positive social communication among our members in the Faculty of Engineering.
